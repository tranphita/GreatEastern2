January 24 Qualifiers

Qualification for GELAC Convention 2025 will be based on Achievers Credits from 1 December 2023 to 30
November 2024 (12 months qualifying period). Credits will be given to every dollar of FYC based on the factor table. Please refer to Circular: 001/24/DAE/EVENTS for further details on calculation of Achievers Credits.
